Our Journey

Explore the key milestones of CarShop that shaped us into who we are today.

Founded in 2010

CarShop began with a mission to transform the car buying experience into something transparent and customer-friendly.

First 100 Cars Sold in 2012

By 2012, CarShop had successfully sold over 100 cars, establishing our presence in the industry.

Nationwide Expansion in 2015

In 2015, we expanded our services across the country, bringing CarShop to major cities nationwide.

AI Integration in 2020

In 2020, we integrated AI-powered car matching, enhancing the customer experience by helping them find the perfect car.

100,000 Cars Sold in 2023

2023 marked a significant milestone as CarShop celebrated selling 100,000 cars, reinforcing our leadership in the market.

Future of CarShop

We continue to innovate and look forward to expanding further while maintaining our customer-first approach.
